- Full name

**By submitting this information, you expressly and voluntarily consent** to BhummiVastu collecting and using it for the sole purpose of delivering the consultation service you have requested.

When you visit our website, certain usage data is collected automatically, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This data is collected via Google Analytics (GA4) and is used solely to understand how our website is used and to improve the user experience.

Our website uses cookies for essential functionality and analytics. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function correctly as a result.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The personal information you provide is used exclusively for:

- **Delivering your consultation** — preparing your Vastu analysis, birth chart, or other booked service
- **Scheduling and communication** — confirming your appointment, sending session details, and follow-up support
- **30-day follow-up support** — responding to queries raised within the follow-up period included in your consultation package
- **Service improvement** — understanding client needs to improve consultation quality (in anonymised, aggregate form only)

We do **not** use your personal information for unsolicited marketing without your consent, and we do **not** sell or trade your personal data to any third party.

## 4. Sensitive Astrological Data

Birth date, time, and place of birth are considered sensitive personal data used exclusively for astrological analysis. You share this information **voluntarily and for the specific purpose** of receiving the consultation service you have booked. This data is treated with strict confidentiality and is never shared, disclosed, or transferred to any third party without your explicit written consent.

## 5. Data Sharing

BhummiVastu does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service delivery:** With tools or platforms (e.g., Zoom, Google Meet) strictly necessary to conduct your consultation session.
- **Analytics:** Anonymised, aggregate usage data is processed by Google Analytics to improve our website.
- **Legal obligation:** If required to do so by applicable law or a valid order from a competent Indian court or government authority.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to deliver your consultation and follow-up support, and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes.
